My top 3 movies of all-time

The Matrix

I love this movie because of it's deeper meanings and its connections between reality and Science-Fiction.

Ready Player One

This movie is among my top three because of its pop culture references and depiction of video gaming in virtual space.

Pokemon: The Movie 2000

This movie helps me to remember the exciting experience of getting my golden Mew card and spending time with my mother and my cousins at a local movie theater.
